About Larry's Lamps

Larry's Lamps specializes in building one-of-a-kind upcycled artistic lamps out of vintage lamp parts, everyday items, and found objects. Larry focuses on steampunk and dabbles in other styles. His lamps tend more toward artistic creations than simple assemblages. The lamps below illustrate a few of the hundreds made over the years. His lamps are sold only through galleries and specialized retail outlets and are unavailable online. Larry started in galleries in Stockon, California, and now creates in Saint Louis, Missouri. Inventory in retail outlets frequently varies.
